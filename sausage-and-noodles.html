Sausage and Noodles

This dish is a simple and quick dinner that is quite delicious

Ingredients

  • Spicy Italian Sausage
  • Penne Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Premix Cheese Blend

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage in a large frying pan, seasoning to taste
  2. In a seperate pot cook the noodles until 90% finished
  3. Once the sausage is cooked add tomato sauce to the frying pan and turn the element to low
  4. Add your noodles to the sauce and stir until combined
  5. Add the premixed cheese pan and stir until combined
