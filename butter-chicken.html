Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a traditional Indian dish that is absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • Butter Chicken Sauce (pre-made or home made, whichever is easier)
  • Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Long Grain Rice (jasmine works well)
  • Naan Bread (garlic naan goes very well with this dish)

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in a slow cooker with the butter chicken sauce and let cook
  2. Cook the rice
  3. cook the naan
  4. Assemble when all ingredients are finished
