Lumberjack Slam

The lumberjack slam is one my favorite dennis meals and one of my go to's when i'm seriously hungry

Ingredients

  1. Two Eggs (cooked to your preference)
  2. One slice of ham
  3. Three slices of Bacon
  4. Three sausage links
  5. Potatoe Hash
  6. Two slices of bread (your preference)
  7. Two buttermilk pancakes

Steps

  1. Cook the above ingredients to your liking
  2. Assemble the ingreedients onto your plate and enjoy!
